About R&W

We are a platform that helps restaurant goers who want to share their opinions about their dining experiences. Our goal is to create a community where food lovers can discover new places and make informed decisions based on genuine feedback.

Some of these dining experiences are great!

And some of them, maybe not so great.

We believe every experience, good or bad, contributes to a more transparent and helpful dining landscape for everyone.

Imagine sharing that does not change whether the experience is good or not. Your honest thoughts and feedback on those experiences.

That's where Kate & Win as a platform helps you to share your experiences about all kind of dining for both you and mostly restaurants.

The more you share your experiences with different restaurants, the more valuable our platform becomes and the more you earn as a part of using this platform.

We strive to make sure your feedback is heard and that it makes the experience you had, even if it's not good.
